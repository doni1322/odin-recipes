Taco

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup converted rice

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir beef and onion until beef is browned, about 5 minutes.
  2. Drain. Stir tomatoes, water, rice, and taco seasoning into beef mixture and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender, about 25 minutes. Top with Mexican cheese blend and lettuce before serving.
