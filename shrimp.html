Shrimp

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 16 large shrimp
  • 3 large cloves garlic
  • 2 teaspoons seafood seasoning

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-low heat until warm, 2 to 3 minutes. Add shrimp, garlic, and pepper flakes all at once and stir together.
  3. Cook and stir until shrimp are bright pink on the outside and the meat is opaque, 3 to 5 minutes.
